Please read before planning around these dates. This calendar is a planning aid, not a guarantee. Dates in the Confirmed section were published by the funder; dates in the Forecast section are our estimates based on when a funder has opened its cycle in past years. We assume grant cycles stay consistent — but this is a volatile moment in philanthropy. Funders may launch new programs, shift deadlines, pause a cycle, or end a program entirely with little or no notice, and federal funding disruption is reshaping many funders' priorities. Always verify directly with the funder before you invest time in an application. Inclusion here is not an endorsement, and this is not a complete list of every opportunity available.
